Once upon a time, there was a farm. On the farm, there were many animals. There was a big pig, a little duck, and a crazy chicken. They all lived together and played all day.
One day, the big pig said, "Let's test who can run the fastest!" The little duck and the crazy chicken agreed. They all stood in a line and got ready to run.
"Go!" shouted the big pig. The little duck, the big pig, and the crazy chicken ran as fast as they could. The crazy chicken was very fast and won the race. The big pig and the little duck were happy for their friend.
After the race, the three friends went back to the farm. They were tired but happy. They knew that they were all special and loved each other very much. And they lived happily ever after on the farm.

Once upon a time, there was a funny cat named Sam. Sam loved to supply his friends with jokes to make them laugh. One day, Sam was worried because he could not think of a new joke.
Sam went to his friend, a little bird named Lily. He said, "Lily, I am worried. I can't think of a new joke to make our friends laugh. Can you help me?"
Lily thought for a moment and said, "Why don't we ask our friends to help us? Maybe they have a joke we can share."
Sam and Lily went to their friends, and they all shared their best jokes. Sam was no longer worried. Now, he had many jokes to supply and make everyone laugh. And they all had a fun and happy day together.

Once upon a time, in a small town, there was an obedient dog named Spot. Spot loved to play with his best friend, a little boy named Tim. They played all day and had lots of fun.
One day, Tim and Spot found an onion in the garden. Tim said, "Let's plant the onion and see what happens." Spot was excited and wagged his tail. They dug a hole and put the onion in it. Every day, they watered the onion and waited for it to grow.
After a few weeks, the onion grew big and tall. Tim and Spot were very happy. They picked the onion and showed it to their friends. Everyone was amazed by the big onion. Tim said, "See, when we work together and take care of things, they can grow big and strong."
And from that day on, Tim and Spot learned the importance of teamwork and taking care of the things they loved.

"Look, Mom! I cut a heart for you because I love you," said Lily with a big smile. Her mom was very happy and gave her a big hug. "I love you too, Lily," her mom said.
The next day, Lily and her mom went to the park. They saw a little boy crying. Lily went to the boy and asked, "Why are you sad?" The boy said, "I lost my toy." Lily wanted to help, so she took out her heart shape and gave it to the boy. "Here, this is for you. I hope it makes you feel better." The boy smiled and said, "Thank you, Lily. I love it!"

Once upon a time, in a hot and sandy land, there was a little camel named Cally. Cally lived near an oasis, a place with water and trees. She loved to play there with her friends.
One day, a fierce lion came to the oasis. He was very big and scary. The lion roared and demanded, "Give me all your food!" Cally and her friends were scared, but they wanted to save their oasis.
Cally had an idea. She told her friends to make a big, loud noise. They stomped their feet and yelled together. The lion was scared of the noise and ran away. Cally and her friends saved the oasis and were very happy.
